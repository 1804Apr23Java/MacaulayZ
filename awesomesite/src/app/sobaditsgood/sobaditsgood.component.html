So bad, they are awesome!

Many filmmakers get their starts with horror movies because they are an easy entry into the film industry. Even though a lot of garbage is released, there is something to be said for sitting around laughing at a bad movie. Even though they might not win any awards, some movies are great to put on, grab a beer, and laugh at the costumes and silly acting. Here is a selection of our favorites.
